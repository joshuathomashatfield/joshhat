Bio

Brief Bio

Joshua Hatfield is a computer science researcher from Huntington, West Virginia. He is completing an M.S. in Computer Science at Marshall University and will begin a Ph.D. in Computer Science at Colorado School of Mines with a focus on human-computer interaction.

His research centers on adaptive simulation environments, VR and XR training, reinforcement-learning-inspired feedback, data-driven telemetry, embedded systems, and human-centered AI. He has worked across VR industrial maintenance training, agent-based community resilience modeling, FPGA-based secure entropy generation, and ML systems optimization.
