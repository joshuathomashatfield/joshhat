Bio

Brief Bio

I am a computer science researcher interested in human-computer interaction, immersive learning environments, adaptive feedback, and AI-supported training systems. My current work explores how VR and intelligent feedback can support procedural learning in safety-critical industrial maintenance settings.

My broader interests include HCI, VR/AR, human-centered AI, spiritual care technologies, narrative systems, and computational models for community resilience.
